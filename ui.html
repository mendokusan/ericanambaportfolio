UI Design Project


Tools and applications

For this Assignment, I decided to attempt making a photo editing software using Photoshop and Illustrator.

Why did I choose them?

The reason I chose photoshop and Illustrator is because of the ease of using them compared to software similar to them and also because it is possible for the to reach each others files. I made my wireframes in Illustrator and mockups in Photoshop and since i based my mockups off my wireframes this made it easier for me.

Tools and Features used Tools and Features used 

Fot the wireframes I mostly used the shape tool,text tool and pen tool and for the mockups I used the adjustment tools, move tool,shape tool,color tool,pen too, text tool,pen tool and transform feature.

Why Do I believe The Way The Tools Were Used Was Competenrt

I believe the way i used the tools made it easier for me to work with them. Also I felt like I took advantage of some tools like transform since it made it easy for me to rotate my shape. Also some of the custom shape tools were quite helpful because I was able to find some shapes that I could use for mockups without attempting to draw them.(The undo and redo icon)

Also I feel like drawing the wireframes in Illustrator and transferring them to photoshop to make the mockups was a good idea since drawing wireframes is much easier with Illustrator at least for me.
