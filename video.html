Video Project


Tools and applications

The tools and applicaions used for creating this video were openshot,gimp and a site named video stabilize. I also used freesound to look for sounds to use in my video.

Why did I choose them?

I was originally planning to use filmora.However, I ended up having to switch it because I realized it had a watermark so I tried Video Pad, Adobe Premiere pro,Adobe Premiere rush and OpenShot. OpenShot had a very simple interface and I found it easier to work with so I ended up using it.I also needed to use Gimp as well since I needed to add a caption in my video. The video stabilizer was mainly used to reduce shakiness of video.

Tools and Features used Tools and Features used 

For making the video, I used OpenShop to add images,video clips and sound in it to create my video. I also found the cutting, effects,sound control,speeding and title tool helpful to improve my video even more. As for Gimp I just used it to write a text so I could put it over my video and the video stabilizer was mainly an online tool used to upload and stabilize a video. I also tried Adobe Premiere pro but it was very slow so I decided to do it online. I also used my phone and my friends phone and a small stand to film my video and take multiple shots.

Why Do I believe The Way The Tools Were Used Was Competenrt

Considering it was a simple review I decided to keep it simple. I do believe that adding a caption and intro was a good idea because it gave more life to the vide. The most useful thing in my opinion was the stabilizer because it really helped me stabilize my video to some degree since my videos were too shaky.

Exported File

My file is exported as an mp4 video format which is the most commonly used one then it was uploaded to youtube as a private video.

Managing Assets

I managed assets by saving multiple copies of cut and edited videos so i could keep them. I also made sure to store them in both my phone,laptop and also an online app named telegram which I used to transfer the files with.


Design Decisions

Using Design Principals and Elements

According to this site, I have made sure that my video’s title stands out so it could look better. I also made sure to make the fonts used in the video easy to read.

Another thing I did was trying to be interactive infront of the camera but it was a little harder than I thought it would be.

Consistency

The designs are consistent mainy because most of review videos tend to get close shots of food and also have the person mostly eating the food and giving details about it.

Design Descisions and Aesthetics

The video was very short so I tried to not add too much to it so it could be kept in topic. I also added multiple shots both side and center close-ups  of the food so the focus would also be on the food as well. Just to make it slightly more interesting I added a caption around the beggining of the video and I also added a sound at the beggining.
