Tools and applications

The tools and applicaions used for creating this animation was mostly Adobe Animate. However, I also user   Online Audio Converter and  freesound.

Why did I choose them?

I chose it mainly due to Adobe Animate being the safest option and not being able to find goo alternatives for it online. I used the online converter so I could convert my wav sound to mp3 so I could decrease the size since that was an issue for me and I used free sound to find source free background music for my animation.

Tools and Features used Tools and Features used 

In animate I used many different things. I tried exploring a lot of options. The functions I have used were motion tweens, clockwise rotation from the rotation menu, sound settings,thr drawing tools and frames.  

 

Why Do I believe The Way The Tools Were Used Was Competent

I believe they were competent due to having the ability to get the work done which was originally my main goal. My animation was simple due to lack of skills in digital art so it did what I wanted it to do.
